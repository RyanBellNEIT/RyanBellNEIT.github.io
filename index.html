About Me

Hi my name is Ryan Bell and I am a game developer/software engineer. I grew up in Lynn, Massachusetts but decided to attended college out of state in pursuit of my education and interest in video games. I studied game development for a total of 3 years at New England Institute of Technology in East Greenwich, Rhode Island. Currently, I have my bachelors degree in Game Development and Simulations Programming. By the end of my time at college, I gained a lot of experience and learning about game development, programming, and got to practice a large variety of programming languages and development styles. I hope to pursue a career in the game development field and further push my education and practice in the field.
